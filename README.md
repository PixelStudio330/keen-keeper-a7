# 👥 KeenKeeper — Tend to Your Inner Circle

> **"Your personal shelf of meaningful connections. Browse, tend, and nurture the relationships that matter most."**

KeenKeeper is a dedicated friendship management system designed to help you stay consistent with the people who matter. Built with a cozy-web aesthetic, it transforms relationship management into a beautiful, tactile experience.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🪴 The Friendship Shelf (Home)
A bird's-eye view of your circle. Cards change color based on contact status (**On-track**, **Almost Due**, or **Overdue**), acting as a gentle visual reminder to reach out.

### ⏳ Interactive Timeline
A complete history of your digital and physical interactions.
* **Search:** Find specific friends or interaction types instantly.
* **Filter:** View only Calls, Texts, or Videos.
* **Smart Sort:** Toggle between Newest and Oldest memories.

### 📊 Friendship Analytics
Visualize your social energy through interactive Pie Charts (via Recharts), showing you exactly how you prefer to stay in touch.

## 📱 Responsive Experience
KeenKeeper is fully optimized for the modern web:
- **Desktop:** High-density 4-column grid for large monitors.
- **Tablet:** Adaptive layouts for seamless browsing.
- **Mobile:** A specialized hamburger menu and "thumb-friendly" touch targets for managing friends on the go.
